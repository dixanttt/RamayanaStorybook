About the Project

This interactive storybook brings the ancient Indian epic Ramayana to life through a combination of text, images, and user interaction. Navigate through different sections of the story, enjoy beautiful illustrations, and immerse yourself in the world of Rama, Sita, and their adventures. Key features of this project include:

  • Interactive storytelling experience
  • Beautiful illustrations for each scene
  • Character dialogues with typing effect
  • Easy navigation between story sections
  • Dark mode for comfortable reading
